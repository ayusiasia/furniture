Natural cotton Sofa SF0243

Model No.:SF0243

 

  1. Natural cotton and linen, skin-friendly and breathable

Cotton and linen fabrics are comfortable, breathable, skin-friendly, and have anti-mite, anti-static and other properties. The contrasting stroke design with orderly transitions and smooth lines increases the sense of spatial hierarchy.

 

 

 

2.Full, soft and elastic sitting feeling

The interior is filled with 45D high-elastic sponge and down, making it comfortable and elastic to sit on, and firmly supported to ensure softness, looseness and support at the same time: soft but not collapsed, with a full sense of wrapping

 

 

3.Removable and washable design

The entire set of fabrics is removable and washable, and the inside is fixed with Velcro. It is easy to install and clean. The slim armrest design, simple lines + naturally drooping skirt, makes it lazy and light.
